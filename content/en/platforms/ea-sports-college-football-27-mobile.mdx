  <Step num="1" title="Define Program Identity">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Allocate Dynasty Points">
    Divide your annual budget between **Coaching Staff**, **Facilities**, and **NIL**. Investing in facilities improves player progression, while NIL is essential for high-stakes recruiting battles.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Scout and Offer Scholarships">

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Manage Roster Retention">
