To successfully launch your coaching career, follow this step-by-step program setup guide:

  <Step num="1" title="Select Your Program and Review AD Expectations">

Decide whether you want to take over a powerhouse with championship-or-bust pressure, or lead a patient, long-term rebuild.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Allocate Your Dynasty Blueprint Points">
    At the start of every season, your program is awarded a set budget of **Dynasty Points** [1]. You must manually distribute these points across coaching staff, athletic facilities, and Name, Image, and Likeness (NIL) funds [1]. A balanced budget is recommended, but you can skew your points heavily toward NIL if you need to secure immediate transfers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Hire and Optimize Your Coaching Staff">
    During the preseason window, recruit coordinators and support staff that complement your playbook [1]. Support staff slots are directly tied to your school's **Team Prestige**, capping out at five slots for elite programs [1]. Coordinators boost on-field performance, while support staff improve off-field operations and injury recovery.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Preseason Scouting and NIL Recruiting">
    Scholarship offers officially open in Week 0 [1]. Allocate recruiting NIL funds to scout prospects, unlock pipeline bonuses, and pitch playing time [1].   <Step num="5" title="Manage Roster Retention and Transfer Risk">
    During the post-season recap, evaluate your current roster. You must allocate **Roster NIL** to key starters to prevent them from entering the Transfer Portal or declaring early for the draft [1].   </Step>
