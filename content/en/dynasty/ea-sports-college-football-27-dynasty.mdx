<Section icon={Users} title="Advanced Recruiting and NIL Mechanics">

Recruiting in **ea sports college football 27 dynasty** has moved beyond simple phone calls. The introduction of **Recruiting NIL** means every scholarship offer is a financial negotiation. Scholarship offers now begin in **Week 0**, and scouting is your primary focus during the preseason.

| Offer Type | Cost Multiplier | Influence Bonus | Best For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Base Offer** | 1.0x | Standard | Core roster depth |
| **Competitive** | 1.5x | High | 4-star prospects |
| **Max Influence** | 2.0x | Maximum | Elite 5-star targets |
| **Retention NIL** | Variable | Prevents Portal | Current star players |

Managing your **Roster NIL** is just as important as signing new talent. During the **End of Season Recap**, you must evaluate which players are at risk of transferring. Increasing a player's NIL allocation reduces their "Transfer Risk" meter, while cutting their NIL budget to save points for recruiting will significantly increase the chance they enter the portal.

<Callout type="warning" title="Transfer Portal Warning">
Transfers place a much higher emphasis on NIL because their recruiting window is significantly shorter than high school prospects. Be prepared to pay a premium for experienced starters.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Program Infrastructure: Facilities and Staff">

Long-term success depends on your **Athletic Facilities grade**. This grade directly affects how quickly your players progress and how attractive your program looks to elite recruits. Facilities range from **Basic (Grade F)** to **National Powerhouse (Grade A+)**.

**Facility Impact on Development:**
- **Equipment Slots**: Higher tiers provide up to 5 equipment slots.
- **Player Health**: Reduces "Wear and Tear" and practice injury chances.
- **Progression Speed**: Boosts the rate at which players earn XP.

Your **Coaching Staff** is the other pillar of infrastructure. You can hire up to five support staff members once your program reaches top-tier prestige. Support staff are specialized; some focus exclusively on reducing practice fatigue, while others provide "pipelines" into specific recruiting territories like Florida or Texas.


| Tier | Title | Equipment Slots | Recruiting Bonus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **F to D** | Basic Facility | 1 | Minimal |
| **C to B** | Competitive | 2-3 | Moderate |
| **A- to A+** | National Powerhouse | 5 | Massive |


<Callout type="tip" title="Staff Management">
Support staff can only be hired or fired during the preseason window. If you miss this window, you are locked into your current staff for the entire year.
</Callout>

</Section>

<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Program Setup Guide">

Follow these steps to ensure your **ea sports college football 27 dynasty** starts on the right path, whether you are taking over a blue-blood program or a struggling underdog.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Analyze AD Expectations">
    Before the season starts, review your Athletic Director's goals. These might include winning a specific rivalry game, hitting a recruiting rank, or maintaining a specific offensive identity. Failing these can lead to a "hot seat" status.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Set the Yearly Blueprint">
    Allocate your Dynasty Points. If you are a small school, put 60% into **Recruiting NIL** to land the talent needed to compete. If you are a powerhouse, invest more in **Facilities** to maintain your edge.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Preseason Scouting">
    In the preseason, use your points to scout as many prospects as possible. Identify "gems" who play above their star rating and "busts" who should be avoided, regardless of their hype.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Week 0 Scholarship Blitz">
    As soon as Week 0 hits, send out your scholarship offers. Use the **2x NIL multiplier** on your top three targets to establish an early lead that other schools will struggle to overcome.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Manage Roster Retention">
    In the final weeks of the season, check your roster's happiness. Use your remaining NIL budget to "lock in" your starters before they have a chance to look at the Transfer Portal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="Automation Options">
