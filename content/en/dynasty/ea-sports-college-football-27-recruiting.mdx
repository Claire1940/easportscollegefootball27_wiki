<Section icon={Users} title="Mastering NIL and Blueprint Strategy">

NIL (Name, Image, and Likeness) is the lifeblood of modern college football recruiting. In EA Sports College Football 27, you must distinguish between Recruiting NIL and Roster NIL to manage your budget effectively.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="Recruiting NIL">
    - Used to attract new prospects.
    - Impacts weekly influence bonuses.
    - Essential for closing 4-star and 5-star battles.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Roster NIL">
    - Used to retain current players.
    - Reduces transfer portal risk.
    - Balances your team's overall depth.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Blueprint Points">
    - Annual budget for all expenses.
    - Must be split across staff and facilities.
    - Requires long-term planning.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

<Callout type="warning" title="Budget Caution">
Do not overspend on Recruiting NIL early in the season. If you exhaust your budget on lower-tier recruits, you will not have the funds to compete for elite talent when they are ready to commit.
</Callout>
