<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Road to Glory Career Builds & Positions">

Road to Glory places a premium on build planning, weekly agenda choices, and on-field execution. Players can craft a fully custom athlete or utilize Legend Templates inspired by icons like Reggie Bush, Vince Young, and Tim Tebow to jumpstart their careers [1].

The mode introduces three highly requested playable positions: **Tight End**, **Edge Rusher**, and **Free Safety** [1]. Your player's physical dimensions dictate their attribute caps, forcing tactical trade-offs between pure speed, acceleration, and physical power.

- **Tight End**: Lean into vertical receiving mismatches or serve as a physical run blocker.
- **Edge Rusher**: Focus on attacking offensive tackles, generating pressure, and disrupting backfields.
- **Free Safety**: Control the deep secondary, limit explosive plays, and force turnovers.

Attribute progression is governed by Max Potential and Cap Breakers. Players can earn up to 25 total Cap Breakers through major milestones like winning the Heisman Trophy, earning All-American honors, or winning national championships. Up to five Cap Breakers can be applied to a single attribute to shatter its original ceiling.

<Callout type="tip" title="Fitness and Composure">
Keep a close eye on your weekly fitness meter. Letting your player's physical readiness drop too low results in temporary rating penalties and decreases coach happiness.
</Callout>
