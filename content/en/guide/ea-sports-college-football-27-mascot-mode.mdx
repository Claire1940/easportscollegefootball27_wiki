<Section icon={Map} title="Introduction to Mascot Mashup">

The **ea sports college football 27 mascot mode**, officially titled Mascot Mashup, is a standalone experience accessible directly from the main menu. Unlike previous iterations where mascot games were hidden within sub-menus, this mode is designed as a primary "party" experience. It balances competitive execution with the "controlled chaos" of oversized heads and giant paws.

In this mode, the traditional roster ratings are discarded. Every single mascot on the field is tuned to a **99 OVR** rating. This levels the playing field, ensuring that matches are decided by stick skills, play-calling, and timing rather than statistical advantages. Whether you are playing as a powerhouse like Georgia or a rising program, your mascots will perform at the highest level.
