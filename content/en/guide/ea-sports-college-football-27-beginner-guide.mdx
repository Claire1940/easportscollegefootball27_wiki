<Section icon={Shield} title="Gameplay Fundamentals and New Controls">

To succeed in **ea sports college football 27**, you must master the updated control schemes. The game introduces more granularity in defensive interactions and passing mechanics, rewarding players who read the field rather than just mashing buttons.

| Control Mechanic | Input Method | Result |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Hit Stick** | Right Stick Up | High-impact tackle / High fumble chance |
| **Wrap Tackle** | Right Stick Right | Safe, fundamental finish |
| **Cut Stick** | Right Stick Down | Attacks the ball carrier's legs |
| **Lunge Tackle** | Right Stick Left | Chasing tackle for difficult angles |

<Callout type="tip" title="Mastering the Catch">
**Timing-Based Catching** adds a green window to your reception attempts. Holding the catch button (Aggressive, Possession, or RAC) and releasing it in the green zone maximizes your chance of a successful catch under pressure.
</Callout>

On the defensive side, **Smarter Coverage Controls** allow you to adjust CB depth, width, and safety midpoints globally. You can now use **Roll Coverage** to shade help toward a specific star receiver or a specific side of the field, countering high-powered offenses that rely on a single primary target.

| Coverage Adjustment | Function | Best Used Against |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Plaster Rules** | Defenders stick to WRs after play extends | Scrambling Quarterbacks |
| **Smart Zones** | Adjusts zone depth based on route spacing | Mesh and Crosser concepts |
| **Double Team** | Brackets a specific receiver | Elite 99 OVR Wide Receivers |
